N'Pure



N'Pure Centella Asiatica Toner is really suitable for those who have acne problems. The toner is light and doesn't make the skin feel sticky. When I use it, it feels really fresh and quickly absorbs into the skin.

One of the main ingredients is Centella Asiatica, which is very famous for calming the skin and reducing redness caused by acne. So, for those who often break out, this toner can really help soothe the skin and speed up the healing of acne.

Apart from that, N'Pure Centella Asiatica Toner also helps control excess oil production on the face. So, the skin feels more balanced and doesn't get oily easily. Plus, this toner also moisturizes the skin well, so even though it helps control oil, the skin remains hydrated.
